Steps showing simplification of [(a + b)(c +d)] .

The expression [(a + b)(c + d)] has two arrows originating from the curly brace under [a + b], and pointing towards the terms [c] and [d] of the binomial. Each term of the expression [(a + b)c + (a + b)d] has two arrows with it. The first set of arrows are originating from [c], pointing towards [a + b].
